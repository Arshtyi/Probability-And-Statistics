考虑视$\left(X_1+X_{n+1}\right),\left(X_2+X_{n+2}\right),\cdots,\left(X_n+X_{2n}\right)$为取自总体$N(2\mu,2\sigma^2)$的简单随机样本，那么样本均值为$\displaystyle\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^n\left(X_i+X_{n+i}\right)=2\overline{X}$,方差为$\displaystyle\frac{1}{n-1}Y$.因为$\displaystyle E\left(\frac{1}{n-1}Y\right)=2\sigma^2$所以

$$
E(Y)=2(n-1)\sigma^2
$$
