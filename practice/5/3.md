## (1)

$\forall i=1,2,3,4,X_i\sim N(0,2^2)$所以

$$
X_1-X_2\sim N(0,8),X_3+X_4\sim N(0,8)
$$

那么

$$
\frac{X_1-X_2}{\sqrt{8}}\sim N(0,1),\frac{X_3+X_4}{\sqrt{8}}\sim N(0,1)
$$

所以

$$
\frac{\left(X_1-X_2\right)^2}{8}+\frac{\left(X_3+X_4\right)^2}{8}\sim\chi^2(2)
$$

因此

$$
C=\frac{1}{8},n=2
$$

## (2)

因为

$$
\frac{\left(X_1-X_2\right)^2}{8}\sim\chi^2(1),\frac{\left(X_3+X_4\right)^2}{8}\sim\chi^2(1)
$$

由$F$分布定义得$\displaystyle Z=\frac{\left(X_1-X_2\right)^2}{\left(X_3+X_4\right)^2}\sim F(1,1)$
