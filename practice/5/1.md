容易知道

$$
\sum_{i=1}^8\left(\frac{X_i}{0.2}\right)^2=\frac{1}{0.2^2}\sum_{i=1}^8X_i^2\sim\chi^2(8)
$$

于是有

$$
P\left\{\sum_{i=1}^8X_i^2<a\right\}=P\left\{\chi^2(8)<\frac{a}{0.04}\right\}=0.95
$$

那么

$$
P\left\{\chi^2(8)>\frac{a}{0.04}\right\}=0.05\Longrightarrow\frac{a}{0.04}=\chi^2_{0.05}(8)=15.507\Longrightarrow a=0.04\times15.507=0.62028
$$
