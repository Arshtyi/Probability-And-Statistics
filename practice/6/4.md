因为总体$X\sim P(\lambda)$故$E(X)=\lambda,D(X)=\lambda$且$E(\overline{X})=E(X)=\lambda,E(S^2)=D(X)=\lambda$，于是$\overline{X},S^2$都是$\lambda$的无偏估计.

当$0\leqslant a\leqslant1$

$$
E\left[a\overline{X}+\left(1-a\right)S^2\right]=a\lambda+\left(1-a\right)\lambda=\lambda
$$

于是$a\overline{X}+\left(1-a\right)S^2$也是$\lambda$的无偏估计.
