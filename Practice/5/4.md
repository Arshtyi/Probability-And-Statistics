容易有

$$
\overline{X}=\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^nX_i\sim N\left(\mu,\frac{\sigma^2}{n}\right)
$$

又由正态分布的性质可得

$$
X_{n+1}-\overline{X}\sim N\left(\mu-\mu,\sigma^2+\frac{\sigma^2}{n}\right)=N\left(0,\frac{n+1}{n}\sigma^2\right)
$$

同时

$$
S^2=\frac{1}{n-1}\sum_{i=1}^n\left(X_i-\overline{X}\right)^2\Longrightarrow\frac{\left(n-1\right)S^2}{\sigma^2}\sim\chi^2\left(n-1\right)
$$

因为

$$
Y=\frac{X_{n+1}-\overline{X}}{S}\sqrt{\frac{n}{n+1}}=\frac{\frac{X_{n+1}-\overline{X}}{\sqrt{\frac{n+1}{n}\sigma^2}}}{\sqrt{\frac{\left(n-1\right)S^2}{\sigma^2\left(n-1\right)}}}
$$

由$t$分布定义知

$$
Y\sim t\left(n-1\right)
$$
